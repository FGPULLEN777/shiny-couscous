You can add any number of application in the ServiceDefinition.csdef</p> <p>You have two option using host headers, or different port numbers. You can try out other combinations (custom dns names etc.)</p> <p>Examples:</p> <p>Host header version:</p> <pre class="prettyprint ">&lt;Sites&gt; &lt;Site name=&quot;Web1&quot; physicalDirectory=&quot;[Path to your app on the dev machine]&quot;&gt; &lt;Bindings&gt; &lt;Binding name=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; endpointName=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; hostHeader=&quot;web1.[cloud service name].cloudapp.net&quot;/&gt; &lt;/Bindings&gt; &lt;/Site&gt; &lt;Site name=&quot;Web2&quot; physicalDirectory=&quot;[Path to your app on the dev machine]&quot;&gt; &lt;Bindings&gt; &lt;Binding name=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; endpointName=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; hostHeader=&quot;web2.[cloud service name].cloudapp.net&quot; /&gt; &lt;/Bindings&gt; &lt;/Site&gt; &lt;/Sites&gt; &lt;Endpoints&gt; &lt;InputEndpoint name=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; protocol=&quot;http&quot; port=&quot;80&quot; /&gt; &lt;/Endpoints&gt;</pre> <p>Using different ports:<br> <br> </p> <pre class="prettyprint ">&lt;Sites&gt; &lt;Site name=&quot;Web1&quot; physicalDirectory=&quot;[Path to your app on the dev machine]&quot;&gt; &lt;Bindings&gt; &lt;Binding name=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; endpointName=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; /&gt; &lt;/Bindings&gt; &lt;/Site&gt; &lt;Site name=&quot;Web2&quot; physicalDirectory=&quot;[Path to your app on the dev machine]&quot;&gt; &lt;Bindings&gt; &lt;Binding name=&quot;Endpoint2&quot; endpointName=&quot;Endpoint2&quot; /&gt; &lt;/Bindings&gt; &lt;/Site&gt; &lt;/Sites&gt; &lt;Endpoints&gt; &lt;InputEndpoint name=&quot;Endpoint1&quot; protocol=&quot;http&quot; port=&quot;80&quot; /&gt; &lt;InputEndpoint name=&quot;Endpoint2&quot; protocol=&quot;http&quot; port=&quot;8080&quot; /&gt; &lt;/Endpoints&gt;</pre> <p>6.
