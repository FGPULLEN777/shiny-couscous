    一般地说，警方可以给被调查对象提供合理的机会（opportunity）给想要犯案的人，例如，要约与毒贩进行毒品交易。但不能引诱人犯下罪行。
    最高法院在 Sorrells v. United States 一案中清楚说明：
    But decoys are not permissible to ensnare the innocent and law-abiding into the commission of crime. When the criminal design originates not with the accused, but is conceived in the mind of the government officers, and the accused is by persuasion, deceitful representation, or inducement lured into the commission of a criminal act, the government is estopped by sound public policy from prosecution therefor.
    而在最高法院在 Matthews v. United States 中为 entrapment defense 定下2个元素：
    (1) Government inducement of the crime, and
    (2) a lack of predisposition on the part of the defendant to engage in the criminal conduct
    且，The central inquiry in entrapment cases is whether law enforcement officials implanted a criminal design in the mind of an otherwise law abiding citizen or whether the government merely provided an opportunity to commit a crime to one who was already predisposed to do so。（United States v. Pennell）
    重点是执法机构是否将犯罪意图植入原本守法的人士中。

    因此，诱捕是有门槛的，一般来说，不用担心警察引诱犯下罪行（这属于 deceitful representation）。而目前投票最多的答案中，他给出的例子足以产生一个 entrapment defense，因而控罪应被撤销。
