Where is the factory reset button on Android 4.0.3?

up vote 2 down vote favorite

I would like to do a factory reset of my Android phone. All guides on the internet I read show a factory reset button under the Privacy tab in Settings. However I don't have this button.

Where is it hidden?

I have Android 4.0.3 on an HTC one phone.

It's easy.

  1. Go to Settings, and then tap Backup & reset.
  2. Tap Reset phone.
  3. If you also want to delete media and other data on HTC One, select Erase all data.
  4. Tap OK.

You can check HTC website also
