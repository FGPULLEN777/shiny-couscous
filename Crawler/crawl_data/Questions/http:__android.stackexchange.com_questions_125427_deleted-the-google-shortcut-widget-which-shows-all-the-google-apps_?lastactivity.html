It isn't a widget, but just a folder where app shortcuts are put together by default. You can create them by just "drag and drop" an icon over another icon and create a folder. Then you can rename it to "Google" or an app category or whatever you like. Look at the screenshots for a more clear understanding on how to bring back the Folder,

Screenshots - Click to enlarge image:

Here's the default folder named Google with Google apps inside it,

Pull Google apps from your Menu drawer by long pressing it and pasting on your home screen. Now merge these two app icons to create a folder.

After merging the two icons it will be displayed as a folder named "Unnamed folder". You can press and edit it's name to "Google".
