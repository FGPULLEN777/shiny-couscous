Built for developers

We’re committed to making the internet a better place. Our products aim to enrich the lives of developers as they grow and mature in their careers.

Built by each of us

We believe that diversity of experiences, perspectives,
and disciplines build better products. We foster an open and
diverse work culture where everyone works together. Every
person we hire makes Stack Exchange an even
better place to work.

Built collectively

We believe great ideas can come from anywhere and welcome anyone to champion ideas everyday. We support a culture that’s open to trying new things and is constantly trying to learn from the things we build.

Built purposefully

Great ideas come from great teams. Our people are our number one asset.
We diligently work at finding new ways to support and encourage
each team member—from compensation to education to supporting
a strong work/life balance.

Stack Benefits

Health & Wellness

We care about you and your family’s health, so we offer the best health, dental, and vision benefits to our US and UK employees. We also offer paid maternity and paternity leave. If you enjoy being active, we offer reimbursement for gym membership and other fitness activities. If you work in one of our high-tech offices, you’ll be treated to a healthy, gourmet lunch every day.

Compensation

We believe every employee should share in our success, so we offer competitive salaries, stock options, and retirement plans with a company match.

Education

We actively encourage continued education. From teams sharing knowledge internally to external training and formal tuition reimbursement, we value a team that’s striving to always be better.

Workspace

We do everything possible to help our teams succeed and feel valued, so we make sure you have the tools and workspaces you need. Ergonomic Aeron chairs and Steelcase desks are standard issue.

Enjoyment

We believe teams perform their best when people enjoy their jobs and the people they work with. We offer multiple ways for team members to interact with each other after-hours, whether you work in the office or remotely.

Work Where You Want

We have offices in New York, London, and Denver.
For many teams and openings, we also offer remote work.
